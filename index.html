Why I built this

When you have to do a task, You make a to do list and track your progress by ticking boxes and feel good that you are consistent, but I believe, Just consistency will not get you anywhere, You have to create output and improve upon the work.

Impact calculator help you do that. Here, You set a goal, You write what you did in your work session, how much you time expected it will take and how much time it took and it will calculate the percentage of work you did. This way, you can understand how much actual work you are doing than just looking like you are busy.
